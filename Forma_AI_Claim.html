Challenge

When claiming becomes a chore

Users struggled with unclear benefit eligibility, and time-consuming manual claiming processes.

Users didn't know what benefits they could use or when they expired

Claims took too long due to manual and recurring submissions

It was hard to find eligible items in the Forma Store

User Problem

Benefits went unclaimed

Users frequently left their benefits unclaimed due to a high-friction, time-consuming process.

Business Problem

Support inquiries increased

The complexity of the manual process led to a surge in support inquiries, straining operational resources.

Solution

How might we help users claim on time and shorten claim time for a better user experience?

Upload mode

AI receipt reader

  1. Pick a benefit category
  2. Simply upload a receipt
  3. System extras data

Subscription mode

System handles repeated processing

  1. Define service duration
  2. Users can set up a repeating pattern instead of resubmit the same claim every cycle
